<h1 id="textbox">
  <p class="alignleft">About Me</p>
  <p class="alignright"><i class="fas fa-user-circle"></i></p> </h1>

<div style="clear: both;"></div>

<p>I am a humanitarian and data scientist, working at the intersection of computer science, economics, and public policy. As a data scientist I uncover signals hidden in complex datasets using machine learning, advanced statistics, mathematical modeling, and rich visualization. As a humanitarian I seek to connect these discoveries to applications that create meaningful change in people&#39;s lives. Through a deeper, richer understanding of human society and history, I seek to give data &#39;a voice&#39; - letting it speak for <em>itself</em> - enabling human action to advance social equality, economic opportunity, and environmental sustainability.</p>
